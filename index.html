C3T is a Dividend-bearing token deployed on TRON, based upon the "Hourglass" contract. Holders of C3T receive Dividends, proportionate to their quantity of tokens. The C3T interface is built with Bootstrap 4, and is based on several different implementations and designs of the "Proof of Weak Hands" Dapp interfaces.
When C3T is purchased, the price of each token rises by a small amount - and when tokens are sold, the price of each token lowers by a small amount. Every token is backed by the ETH stored in the C3T Smart Contract - meaning every token will always have value.

Additionally, every Transfer, Trade, Buy or Sell of C3T has a flat fee of 10%. This fee makes up the ETH which is paid as Dividends to token holders.
There are several strategies you could follow - the best one is the one you form for yourself. When you have C3T, you're earning Dividends. Compound those Dividends into more C3T, and get more Dividends faster - or, withdraw those Dividends to your wallet, and HODL onto your existing C3T.

Whatever you decide is up to you - do keep in mind, the market is constantly active, so the price / value could change at any time - it is up to you when you buy or sell C3T. It is for this reason that you should only invest with funds you are willing to lose.
